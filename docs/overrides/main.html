An AI Game Master for tabletop RPG campaigns.
Open source. Self-hosted. Cheaper than a coffee.

⸻ ✦ ⸻
🎲
Rules in Code
Full SRD 5e combat, spells, conditions, and character progression — implemented as a deterministic Python engine, not prompt engineering.
🧠
Claude as GM
Claude handles the story — narration, NPC dialogue, world reaction, plot twists. The engine handles the math. Each does what it's best at.
💾
Persistent Campaigns
Stop mid-dungeon, come back next week. Every turn is saved. Your campaign survives across sessions, crashes, and server restarts.
👥
Solo & Multiplayer
Play alone or with friends. Real-time multiplayer with initiative-based combat, player presence tracking, and mid-session join/leave.
🎭
Two Ways to Play
Browser-based web client for the full visual experience, or Discord bot with voice support. Both are first-class, both ship with Tavern.
🌍
Community Worlds
Use the included Shattered Coast setting or import community-created world presets. Build and share your own — it's just a Markdown file.
⸻ ✦ ⸻

Three Ways to Gather

🖥️
At Your Desk
Open the web client in your browser. Create a character, start a campaign, and play solo or invite friends with a link.
📱
At the Table
Put a laptop at the head of the table as the shared display. Each player joins on their phone. Claude narrates through the speakers.
🎙️
On Discord
Add the Tavern bot to your server. Join a voice channel, start a campaign, and play — Claude speaks as your GM. Type or talk, your choice.
Cheaper Than a Coffee
Prompt caching, mixed model routing, and rolling summaries keep API costs minimal. A full evening of adventure costs less than what you'd tip your barista. Bring your own Anthropic API key — no subscription, no platform fees.
